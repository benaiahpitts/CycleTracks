Florida Trip Tracker

This mobile app collects information in order to learn more about travel habits. The information will help identify ways to collect travel data in the future. Your participation is greatly appreciated.

Please answer some questions about yourself and use the app to track your travel patterns. The information will then be sent to the Trip Tracker Team for use in developing future travel data collection methods.

Thank you for your participation!
